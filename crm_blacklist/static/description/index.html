Why should you add Blacklist Partner to your Odoo ?

In Odoo users can create and manage partner categories to organize the address book of the company but these tags are only informative. Our "Blacklist Partner" app will allow you to create blacklisting categories.

Features

Blacklisting Tags

Apply one or several blacklisting tags on your partners.

Direct view

See directly in your list of contacts if there are blacklisted elements.

Sale Warning

Warns users which are selecting a blacklisted customer in a Quotation or a Sale Order.
